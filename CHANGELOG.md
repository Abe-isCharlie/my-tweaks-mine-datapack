## 0.0.3 - 2026-03-28

### Added

- Added crafting recipe to convert Nether Quartz Blocks back into Nether Quartz.
- Enabled Nether Quartz recovery using Crushing Wheels from [Create Mod](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/create).

## 0.0.2

### Added

- Added small Ancient Debris clusters to Soul Sand Valley biome using NeoForge biome injector.

### Changed

- Reduced spawn rates of small, medium, and large Ancient Debris clusters for balancing.

## 0.0.1

### Added

- Increased spawn rates of vanilla Ancient Debris clusters in the Nether.
- Added a new medium Ancient Debris cluster spanning layers Y=8 to Y=176.
